5.5/2.1mm DC Power Jack to 2-pin Connector Plug Male Power Supply Cable



  • Description
  • Specifications
  • Warranty & Returns
  • Order

This power jack is designed to fit 5.5mm / 2.1 mm power plugs snugly and securely. Perfect for adding a power connector to your project.

The 2 conductor wire is covered with thick red and black insulation. In the end, the wire is attached to a JST XH 2.54mm 2-pin connector plug male.

Custom length available!

  • 5.5mm / 2.1mm standard DC jack;
  • The center positive wiring (center+);
  • The wires are color coded - red is center, black is ring;
  • JST XH 2.54mm / PH 2.00mm 2-pin connector plug male;
  • The maximum load current does not exceed 3A;
