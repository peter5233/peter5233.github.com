About us

WiiHey is an IoT company that integrates smart sensors, wireless connectivity and AI-based data analysis to provide end-to-end solutions for enterprise digital transformation. We help customers collect and analyze data from connected assets, devices, people, places to gain valuable insights and optimize business model.

For instance, WiiHey's IoT solution of industrial asset monitoring allows users to inspect and maintain their assets without visiting the site. The benefits can be seen in aspects of both preventative and corrective maintenance. Preventative maintenance is conducted before any issues are detected to ensure the system is operating at highest performance, while corrective maintenance is conducted after an issue has been discovered and requires repair. The system sends immediate pinpointed alerts when problem happens, and enables remote troubleshootings to facilitate maintenance. It is even possible to shift some corrective maintenance into preventative maintenance and eliminate the overall maintenance tasks.

At WiiHey, we believe that by making it easy to deploy sensors and collect data, customers of all types will be able to use IoT skills, improve business operations, and unlocking efficiencies like never available before.
